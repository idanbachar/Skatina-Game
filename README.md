# Skatina

## What is Skatina?


**Skatina** is a game that you play as a ball, and your goal is to pass levels
<br/>
that get harder when you level up, with new challanging obstacles that make you angry.
<br/>
Only lucky people can finish the game.

## Rules/How to play

-**Your character** in the game:


| Alive | Dead |
| ------ | ------ |
|<img width="200" src="images/player/player.png" />| <img width="200" src="images/player/player_dead.png" />|

-There are **obstacles with different types** in the game:


| Name/Info | Object/Obstacle |
| ------ | ------ |
| **Purple wall (also can move)** - kills the player in touch | <img width="25" src="images/obstacles/wall_obstacle.png" />
| **Blue regular wall (also can move)** - blocks player movement in touch. | <img width="25" src="images/objects/wall.png" />
| **Purple wall bullets** - kills the player in touch. | <img width="25" src="images/obstacles/bullet.png" />
| **Floor (also can move)** - can make the player fall to death when moves. | <img width="25" src="images/objects/floor.png" />
| **High-jump floor** - makes the player jump to high that can easily make him touch any obstacles around. | <img width="25" src="images/objects/jumping_floor.png" />
| **Finish floor** - finish current level and starting the next one. | <img width="25" src="images/objects/finish_floor.png" />

*The game is hard :)
